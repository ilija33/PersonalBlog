AntennaPod

AntennaPod is one of this best apps for podcast lover. Supports material you and import/export with OPML.

You can add podcasts through RSS feed, search in app or add localy stored podcasts. I use it and like it.

You can find it on playstore f-droid or github.

https://antennapod.org

Innertune

If you are someone who likes to stream music and are annoyed with youtube and spotify try innertune.

This is foss android app that searches songs through youtube and offer free streaming and offline listening.

Only available on github and f-droid.

https://github.com/z-huang/InnerTune

Organic Maps

One of the best google maps alternatives that uses OpenStreetMaps as maps provider.

It offer but you don't need to sign in with OpenStreetMaps account and contribute to app by adding important locations.

One of it's strengths is that it support offline maps.

You can get it on playstore, f-droid, and github.

https://organicmaps.app
